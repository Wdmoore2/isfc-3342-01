Delta Chi and UA Little Rock

The Ua Little Rock Chapter of the Delta Chi Fraternity was established in 2016, making it one of the newest greek organizations on campus

History of Delta Chi

Our brotherhood dates way back to October 13, 1890 when eleven law students at Cornell University formed a Law Fraternity. In 1922, the Delta Chi officially became a social fraternity, thus allowing men in all areas of study to join the brotherhood. In 1929, Delta Chi became the first fraternity to officially abolish hazing practices associated with “hell week.” Since our founding at Cornell, Delta Chi has grown to more than 120 chapters and colonies across the United States and Canada and has initiated more than 107,000 brothers.
